Get to know LOZAR ELECTRIC

About Us

Lozar Electric is a business name for a group of Electrical service renderers based in Florida and work globally.

Lozar Electric is primarily aimed at providing affordable and efficient electrical services. Not necessarily profitable but reliable and pleasant job is what exactly define us.

In 1992, a local apprentice at a local elctric workshop thought about sometthing great while searching for some wires to explore how electricity works, he was doing this and he made a mistake by bridging Life and Neutral cables and BOOM! , all the light in the area went off, the local grid has been affected by the mistake of this young boy.

The boy was not alone in the workshop, his friend was also there with him, They both screamed and ran out of the workshop knowing that they had created a big mess. The apprentice since then vowed to always use quality materials and work ethically whenever he gets a job knowing that the materials used for the electrification in his area are not up to standard and also, the work were not done ethically.

Few years later, he had grown up to be a qualified electrical personnel, he sought for like-minded electrical proffessionals and they teamed up and created the business LOZAR ELETRIC.

L stands for Louis, O stands for Otto, Z stands for Zeus, A stands for Adam, R stands for Reynald and ELECTRIC was added at the back to indicate their area of expertise.
